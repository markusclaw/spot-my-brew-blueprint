Key Decisions

Tech Stack: PostgreSQL + Python FastAPI

Approved

Owner: Rafa

Best fit for rapid development and team expertise. Familiar, well-documented, excellent community.

Deployment Platform: Cloudflare (Pages + Workers)

Approved

Owner: Greg

Fast global CDN, low cost, no vendor lock-in, proven reliability. Aligns with team infrastructure preferences.

MVP Scope: Beer + Ratings + Origins (Web-First)

Approved

Owner: Greg + Rafa

Ruthlessly scoped to launch in 4-6 weeks. Social, badges, mobile native, and monetization deferred to v2+.

Data Model: Relational (PostgreSQL, not NoSQL)

Approved

Owner: Rafa

Beers, breweries, ratings, and locations are relational. ACID compliance matters. Easier to reason about relationships.

Design Language: Casual, Community-Focused Aesthetic

Forming

Owner: Greg

Reflects authenticity of Mexican craft beer culture. Premium but approachable. Not corporate.

Constraints & Boundaries

No Monetization in V1

This is a passion project and community service. No ads, no premium tiers, no sponsored listings. Keep it pure.

Web-First, Mobile-Responsive

MVP is web. Native iOS/Android apps deferred to v2+. Must be fully functional on mobile browsers.

MVP Timeline: 4-6 Weeks

Hard constraint. Ruthless scope enforcement. If a feature doesn't fit, it goes to v2+.

Community-Sourced Data (Initially Manual)

MVP will seed with manual curation of top breweries and beers. User contributions come post-launch as the community grows.

Notable Tradeoffs

✗ Speed vs. Perfection

We're optimizing for launch speed. This means some features are rough and some data is manually managed. Refinement happens post-launch.

✗ Feature Breadth vs. Depth

We're doing beer discovery + ratings exceptionally well, not doing social, badges, or owner portals at all. Deep in core, empty in nice-to-haves.

✗ Open Curation vs. Closed Quality

MVP data is curated by the team (quality > quantity). Community contributions and moderation come later.

✗ Platform Reach vs. MVP Scope

Starting with Mexico. No international expansion in v1. Focus > sprawl.
