ActivePhase 0 (Seed)

Overview

Spot My Brew is a craft beer discovery platform built for Mexico's growing community of beer enthusiasts. The MVP allows users to:

  • Browse a curated catalog of craft beers available in Mexico
  • Rate beers and share reviews with the community
  • Find where to buy specific beers and their origins
  • Discover brewery profiles and backgrounds
  • Search by style, brewery, and region
