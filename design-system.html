Design Philosophy

Spot My Brew's design reflects the authenticity and passion of Mexico's craft beer culture. The aesthetic is casual, community-focused, and genuinely craft—not corporate or pretentious.

Casual & Approachable

Built by beer lovers for beer lovers. Warm, inviting, human. Not stuffy or formal.

Community-Centric

Celebrates user voices and brewery stories. Content and interactions center on the community.

Authentic & Craft

Rustic, tactile, handcrafted feel. Reflects the artisanal nature of craft brewing.

Spacing & Layout

Based on an 8px grid. Generous whitespace creates breathing room and premium feel.

8px: Base unit (padding, gaps, small spacing)

16px: Section padding, component spacing

24px: Larger sections, vertical spacing

32px: Major section separation
